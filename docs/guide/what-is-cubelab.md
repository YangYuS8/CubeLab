# What Is CubeLab

CubeLab is the upper-layer teaching and learning ecosystem that sits on top of CubeSandbox.

Where CubeSandbox is responsible for fast and isolated MicroVM execution, CubeLab is responsible for turning that execution substrate into a structured education product.

## Core Goals

- Model labs as reusable specifications
- Keep sandbox capabilities behind stable abstractions
- Support automated checks and future AI teaching assistance
- Preserve reviewability, safety, and maintainability
